Dr. Alice Miller

Psychiatry & Psychology

I am a board-certified psychiatrist with over 10 years of experience treating patients with mental health disorders. My approach to treatment is to provide a safe and supportive environment for patients to explore their thoughts and feelings. I believe in taking a holistic approach to mental health, which includes medication management, therapy, and lifestyle changes.

Education:

  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology, University of California, Los Angeles
  • Doctor of Medicine from the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF)
  • Psychiatry Residency at Stanford University Medical Center

Specialties:

  • Psychiatric Evaluation and Assessment
  • Medication Management
  • Psychotherapy and Counseling
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)

Experience & Professional Memberships

  • Private Practice Psychiatrist since 2012
  • Clinical Faculty at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) Department of Psychiatry
  • Chief of Psychiatry at City Hospital in San Francisco
  • Consultant Psychiatrist for several community mental health organizations
  • American Medical Association
  • California Psychiatric Association
